About
Me

Hi there! I'm Ashley Cooper, or Cooper for short. I would say I took an unusual path towards design. After graduating with an IT degree I was slowly drawn into a graphic design from a manager who saw my potential. I've always loved drawing and creating designs but never thought I would make it as a graphic designer. I'm proving myself wrong and pushing forward through a graphic design certificate program through the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. I'll graduate from the program this summer.

What's next for me? I'm not sure. I do know that I'll continue to push my design aesthetic and my skills to continue my graphic design education. I'm excited for the next path I take.
