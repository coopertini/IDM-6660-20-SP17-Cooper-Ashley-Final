Ashley Cooper Portfolio Website

Website Design

I wanted the design of my portfolio to be clean and subtle, but with a twist. A portfolio site is a direct reflection on the designer, so I incorporated my personality into every page to show my sense of humor but also a clean style with purpose.

The logo design was also a part of this project. I wanted the logo to express my clean aesthetic and my love for science/technology (element symbol, anyone?). I'm also a sucker for red.

My cat Thor also makes a cameo appearance.
