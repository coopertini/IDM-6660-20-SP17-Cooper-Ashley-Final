Curiosities of the Sky

Print

I took another dive into astrology for this project to design the book Curiosities of the Sky by Garrett P. Serviss. This book was originally published in 1909, and I liked that many of the findings in the book were still relevant to this day. I included images from the Hubble telescope for the chapter introduction spreads to raise the sense of wonder that space always brings me. This was an exercise on print formatting for me – and from my perspective this kind of book needed lots of margin space to leave room for notes and inspiration. I also included my own minimalist illustrations, which give a nice balance to the lush imagery used for the chapter introduction spreads.
