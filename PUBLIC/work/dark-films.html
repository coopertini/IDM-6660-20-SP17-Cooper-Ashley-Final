Dark Films

Branding

This project delves into the dark side as I created a brand for a horror movie film production company. Inspiration for the name of the company came from unlikely objects: teeth and a dagger. I used both of those elements to create the jagged lines used to create the logo, as well as what I refer to as the "web", which was applied to the collateral for the brand.
