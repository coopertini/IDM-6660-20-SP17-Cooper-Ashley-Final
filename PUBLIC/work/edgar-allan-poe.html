Edgar Allan Poe Poster Series

Print

With this set of posters, I explored the dimensions of rich black and process black to see how they printed on top of each other on a Konica printer. I used minimalist patterns with lines to form the shapes of each story, and then overlaid the key word from each to tie it all together.
