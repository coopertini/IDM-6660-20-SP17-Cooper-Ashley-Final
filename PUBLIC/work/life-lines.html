Life Lines

Print

This was a deeply personal project to me as this was essentially a journal through my life’s story. I wanted to show the connection between my line drawings that were a big part of my life from high school through college and how they helped remind me about what I needed during those tough times in my life.

I took all 130 illustrations spanning over 10 years of my life to create this book. The illustrations were all scanned and placed in chronological order along that illustration’s story.

One of my favorite elements from this book is the gradient of all the colors visible when bending the book.
