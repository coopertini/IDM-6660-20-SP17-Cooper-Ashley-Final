Milton Glaser Booklet

Print

I wanted to create a booklet that took Milton Glaser’s style but with elements of my own. I used colors from his famous work for the Mad Men television series poster designs and also leveraged his love for geometric shapes. My design instilments included the type setting and overall formatting of the back poster.

The booklet will open to reveal a poster on the back that includes all of his posters since the 1960s. His famous quote “Art is Work”, which is prevalent above his door at his studio in New York City, is also represented as a feature on the poster.
