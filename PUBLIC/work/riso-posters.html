Riso Posters

Print

This was a found object exercise for myself. I’ve never been to the store Hunt and Gather before this project, but oh my what fun it is to see the most eclectic arrangement of items that have nothing to do with each other besides the fact that they are all old. I stumbled across a pile of small cards, whether they were elements to a game, from a deck or a smaller piece of collateral from nothing in particular. I scanned those images to create layered posters that were then printed on a Risograph printer that displays colors like screen printing. These are the results.
