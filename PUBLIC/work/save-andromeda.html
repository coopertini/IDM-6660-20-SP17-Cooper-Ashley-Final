Save Andromeda Winery

Branding

I had an opportunity to combine two passions of mine with this project: looking at the stars and drinking wine. Save Andromeda Wine was created from a love for constellations and the stories that they tell. I incorporated the stories I loved with the wines that I appreciated.

Using colors inspired by the northern lights I captured hues that literally came from the sky. And by adding in the silhouettes of the animals from each constellation it gives the bottles a contemporary design compared to the usual suspects on your liquor store shelves. Having white as the base for the bottles was also intentionally done to make these stand out to potential buyers.
