Toe Vinyl

Branding

This instrumental band from Japan had atmospheric tones and bright but deep melodies. I wanted to create a vinyl that matched with that aesthetic. I created the background of the vinyl by taking broken CD pieces and shining light through to invoke the prism of colors. I layered that with a texture of warped mailbox numbers to give the image an undertone pattern.
